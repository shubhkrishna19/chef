# Quarterly Investors Report
**FY 2025-26 | Quarter 3**

## Financial Snapshot
| Metric | Q3 FY 2025-26 (Current) | Q3 FY 2024-25 (Previous Year) | YoY Growth |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Revenue** | ₹ 15.4 Cr | ₹ 9.2 Cr | +67% |
| **Gross Margin** | 42% | 35% | +7% pts |
| **EBITDA** | ₹ 2.8 Cr (18%) | ₹ 0.9 Cr (10%) | +211% |
| **Net Profit** | ₹ 1.9 Cr | ₹ 0.4 Cr | +375% |

## Financial Pointers
*   **Revenue Surge**: Achieved highest-ever quarterly revenue driven by strong festive season sales and successful launch of the "Modern Living" collection.
*   **Margin Expansion**: Gross margins improved significantly due to bulk procurement efficiencies and in-house manufacturing of key components (reducing reliance on imports).
*   **EBITDA Growth**: Operational leverage kicked in; while marketing spend increased for the festive push, it yielded a 4x ROAS, significantly boosting the bottom line.
*   **Cash Flow**: Maintained positive operating cash flow; reinvested surplus into machinery upgrades (see Innovations).

## What we have achieved in Last Quarter
*   **Sales Milestones**: Crossed the 10,000 units sold mark for the quarter.
*   **Channel Expansion**: Successfully onboarded 2 major online marketplaces, resulting in a 30% uplift in daily order volume.
*   **Operational Excellence**: Reduced average order fulfillment time from 7 days to 4 days.
*   **Customer Satisfaction**: NPS score improved from 55 to 72 following the revamp of our post-sales support team.

## What's the Plan Ahead / Under Development
*   **Retail Footprint**: Planning to launch 2 flagship experience centers in Bangalore and Mumbai by Q1 FY27.
*   **Tech Integration**: Developing a proprietary "AR-Try-On" feature for our D2C website to reduce returns and improve conversion rates.
*   **Sustainability Initiative**: Transitioning packaging to 100% recyclable materials by the end of next quarter.

## People added into the team
*   **Head of Supply Chain**: Hired an industry veteran with 15 years of experience to streamline logistics and warehousing.
*   **Creative Director**: Onboarded to lead the new "Luxe" sub-brand design language.
*   **Performance Marketing Lead**: To optimize our growing digital ad spend and drive CAC down further.

## Product Strategy
*   **Category Expansion**: Entering the "Home Office" segment with ergonomic chairs and smart desks, targeting the hybrid work demographic.
*   **Premiumization**: Launching a "Signature Series" using premium hardwoods (Teak/Walnut) to capture higher ticket sizes (ASP > ₹25k).
*   **Customization**: Pilot launch of "Build Your Own" module for wardrobes and shelving units.

## Innovations
*   **Automated Production**: Commissioned the new 5-axis CNC machine which has increased production speed by 40% for complex joinery.
*   **Material Tech**: Successfully tested a lightweight, high-strength composite board for our budget range, reducing shipping weight by 20%.
*   **Finishing**: Implemented a UV-curing finishing line that reduces drying time from 24 hours to 15 minutes, significantly reducing WIP inventory.
